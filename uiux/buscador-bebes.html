SCROLL UNTIL THE BOTTOM TO DISCOVER SOME BABY STEPS


CASE STUDY:

Design a way future moms could find ideas (and their meaning) to name their newborns, more than a "name finder" it should be a "name discovery tool, too"



SOLUTION:
The solution I provided was to divide the service in two steps:
1) If the user has already a name in mind and wants to know the meaning of it
2) The option to randomize some names by choosing only the beginning letter
Also, it was important to consider the gender of the newborn and where the name is from, any of this options is an "OR" selection
