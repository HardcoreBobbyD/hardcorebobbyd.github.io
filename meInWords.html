MY PERSONA

Nicknames are considered to be alter egos of a kind, well mine is not. And not to blow my horn, but I am as my name reads--Diamond.

Sharply cut, angled with precision, and shinny. That's how my work is produced, and though some of my ideas may still need to be processed and polished, they are diamonds nonetheless.

I’ve been obsessed for the visual aesthetics for the last 10 years, at this point in my life i'm focusing my career in the UX/UI & Product Design.

I’m curious driven, always.
